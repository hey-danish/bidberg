Company History

Our journey began as a Product Company, where our successful implementations generated significant traction among stakeholders. However, embracing transformation, we have emerged anew in the field of Information Technology, now offering a fresh array of innovative solutions and services.

2019

2023

2019 (Q1)

The inception of the innovative "eAuction As a Service" platform took place during this period, marking the introduction and successful implementation of this concept in the Indian market for the first time

2019 (Q2)

The development of the MVP/PoC for the Idea commenced with a select group of developers

2019 (Q3)

The MVP had reached a stage where it could be presented to potential investors

2019 (Q4)

Renowned investors and startup acceleration bodies such as T-Hub, Angel Investors, Global Mooly, and others showed significant interest in the MVP by the end of 2019, propelling its traction

2020 (Q1)

At this juncture, the Bidberg Platform (eAuction as a Service) was launched and actively promoted to stakeholders including brands, celebrities, NGOs, and users

2020 (Q2)

Multiple queries and notable attention were received from stakeholders, leading to the successful onboarding of a few of them

2020 (Q3)

Due to the need to adapt to the evolving dynamics of the business model, it became necessary to change the Line of Business (LOB) of the establishment. This change resulted in the temporary pause of onboarding activities, and eventually, due to market shifts, the operational existence of the establishment ceased

2020 (Q4)

Following an exciting journey, we have officially taken the platform offline

2023 (Q2)

Once again, we have undergone a transformation, reemerging with a diverse range of service lines around Information Technology such as Software Development, IT Consulting, Web Hosting, and Coaching
