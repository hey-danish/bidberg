About Us

Empowering the Digital Age

Bidberg, was founded in 2019, in Hyderabad, as a product based Startup. The Company's journey began as a Product Company, where we successfully implemented innovative eAuction As A Service platform in the Indian market for the very first time.

Our Bidberg platform generated significant traction among stakeholders and secured seed funding proposals for acceleration from renowned investors such as T-Hub, Angel Investors, Global Moolya, and others. The propelling tractions from the community leads us to close some successful onboarding of Celebrities.

Due to the need to adapt to the evolving dynamics of the business model, it became necessary to change the Line of Business (LOB) of the establishment. This change resulted in the temporary pause of onboarding activities, and eventually, due to market shifts, the operational existence of the establishment ceased by the end of 2020. The product went off the public domain following this short exciting journey.

However, embracing transformation, we have emerged anew in the field of Information Technology, now offering a fresh array of innovative solutions and services.
